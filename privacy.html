Privacy Policy — La Ruota

Developer: Michele Schiavo / Direktunes
App name: La Ruota
Last updated: April 2026

No data collection

La Ruota does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information about its users.

No user accounts

The app does not require or offer user registration, sign-in, or accounts of any kind.

No internet access

La Ruota works completely offline. It makes no network requests and has no connection to any external server, database, or third-party service.

No analytics or advertising

The app contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporters, no advertising networks, and no tracking of any kind.

Device permissions

La Ruota does not request access to the camera, microphone, location, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive device resource. It uses only the vibration motor (for haptic feedback) and audio settings (to control sound playback).

Entertainment purposes only

La Ruota is an entertainment app. The messages displayed by the app are randomly generated for amusement purposes only and do not constitute advice of any kind.

Children's privacy

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages including children under 13 (COPPA compliant).

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The app will not change its data-collection behaviour without an update to this policy.
