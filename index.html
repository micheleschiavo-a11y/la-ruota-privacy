Privacy Policy

App: La Ruota  |  Last updated: March 30, 2026

La Ruota is a fortune-wheel entertainment app developed by Michele Schiavo / Direktunes. This Privacy Policy explains what information (if any) is collected when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

La Ruota does not collect any personal information. The app does not require you to create an account, does not ask for your name, email address, location, or any other personal data.

2. Data Storage

All data (such as your language preference and the wheel's last position) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server or third party.

3. Analytics & Tracking

La Ruota does not use any analytics service, advertising SDK, or tracking technology. We do not track your behaviour inside or outside the app.

4. Third-Party Services

La Ruota does not integrate any third-party services (such as Facebook, Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar). The app runs entirely on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

La Ruota does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for all age groups.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will be changed accordingly.
